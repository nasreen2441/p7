Flowers

Flowers are the reproductive parts of a plant. They are not only involved in reproduction but are also a source of food for other living organisms.There are countless types of flowers, but broadly speaking, they can be classified into four main groups:

  1. Angiosperms (flowering plants)

  2. Gymnosperms (conifers and cycads)

  3. Mosses

  4. Ferns
